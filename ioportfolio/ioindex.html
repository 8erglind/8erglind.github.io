Let's Talk About the Weather

This movie is about my dad and climate change. It is part of one of my latest projects called Let´s Talk About The Weather.
It is very difficult to believe in something that is not visible yet. To do so we need mediators, like a weather reporter. A weather reporter talks about the future; a place, a time and a sensation that does not exist yet. At the same time they also act as mediators of climate change. Lets talk about the weather focuses on our relationship with weather, but also my relationship to my father. This is an intuitive exploration on how climate change manifests itself.
The short film will be screened in a programme about climate change in San Mei Gallery in London. The programme is created by the It´s Freezing in LA, a new independent magazine printing fresh insight & brilliant illustrations on climate change. (https://www.itsfreezinginla.co.uk/)
So join the venture between San Mei Gallery and It's Freezing in LA! Tomorrow between 2-9pm 28th March!
Hope to see you there!
